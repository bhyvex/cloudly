Get your servers into shape! 100% free and open-sourced.

Project Cloudly is an enterprise grade Real-time IT Infrastructure and Applications Monitoring platform developed under the Open Source model.

Cloudly is 100% Free and Open Source software, with no license fees, software subscriptions or "special" versions.

Feel free to checkout our code at the GitHub.

Easy to install! It takes less than 5 seconds to get you up and monitoring.

Automatic agent installation for any Unix like system (or Linux); and alternative installer scripts/packages that works in any environment.

The installation literally takes 5 seconds as it requires only a single copy & paste at your server terminal prompt.

Smart Alerting. Get to know about problems before they appear.


Advanced server monitoring alerts.

Get notified of problems before they affect your customers - email, SMS, push notification, web hooks with integrated escalation management.

Graph your infrastructure. Performance anomalies detection.

Investigate alerts with your own real-time graphs dashboards across servers and a multiple data centres. Stack, filter and summarise all your data, kept for 30 days or forever so you can plan and spot trends.

NAGIOS compatible. Works out of the box with Nagios Plugins.

While Cloudly is useful “out of the box”, it is designed to be highly customizable and work with the industry standard Nagios Plugins to create an unique and integrated servers monitoring solution. It does so fully automatically with very little or no interaction with the terminal prompt.

Control your data.
Hosted on your server.

You are fully in control of your data. You decide where your Dashboards are going to be hosted. We support you to install your Dashboard platform on your own server infrastructure or in your preferred data center.
